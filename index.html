Who we are

Food4NJ is a community, not a government agency.

We are volunteers and neighbors who come to Delaney Hall to stand with families. We bring groceries, gift cards, and support. We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit with no connection to ICE, law enforcement, or any government agency.

🛍

Free groceries & gift cards

We pack bags of food and distribute grocery store gift cards to families as they leave visits.

📋

Preserving your story

We record and protect the stories of families affected by detention so the world knows what is happening here.

🤝

No judgment, no reporting

We are here only to help. We will never share your information with authorities.

🌏

All languages welcome

You do not need to speak English. Share your story in any language.

Your safety

Your privacy is our promise.

We understand you may be afraid. Here is exactly how we protect you.

🔒

We will NEVER share your story with ICE, police, or any government agency.

🗑

You can request your video be deleted at any time by emailing us.
